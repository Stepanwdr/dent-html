About Dentapp

DentApp is an online service developed to help dental care seekers have access to high-quality board-certified independent dental care professionals 24/7.

DentApp makes the perfect connection between patients and dentists through its unique teledentistry technology that enables virtual dental visits to improve access, transparency and continuity of care. If you’re a patient looking for virtual dental care or to live chat with a dentist,

DentApp makes it easy. Our technology also makes it simple to find the right dentist near you, make a dental appointment online and consult with specialists from the comfort of your home or workplace.
